Message from Principal

History

Established in 2004 B.S. Saraswati Niketan has served the nation well in terms of spreading the light of education and producing eligible human resources in different fields. The school was initiated by a local lad of ‘Jyapu’ community to educate the young boys of his community in a congested room on the ground floor of his home.

Vision

To be a model school that provides holistic education. To produce intellectually sound and socially responsible citizens who can compete in the global market. Creating a holistic learning enviroment where all learners regardless of their background, race and ethinicity that can foster their inherent capabilities.
